The Jobs Hardest to fill

The first question came to us is how to measure hardness to fill. The date it has been posted might be a good indicator. Using the word might, we mean it could be possible they have found person to fill this position but they did not remove the most, which is totally possible.The 25% quantile of posted days is 24 and 75% is 129, which is quite reasonable. If the office remove the job post once this position is filled, we could say that the hardest to fill job is Account Manager (Job ID 87990), which is spectable.

Generally speaking, the position with lower salary and higher demanding is hard to fill. Demanding might be experience, skill set, degree, residency.etc. Using reggular expression, we extracted the number of experience each jobs needs. This graph there is an positive linear relation between experience and salary. Those position under the regressin line might be the hardest to fill. From this perspective the hardest to fill job is Excutive Director, Business Development(JobID:97899)
